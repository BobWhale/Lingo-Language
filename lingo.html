The method

Built for the way
the mind learns.

Lingo was designed with cognitive scientists and polyglots to replicate the conditions of natural language acquisition — without moving abroad. Every session is adaptive. Every word is placed at exactly the moment you need it.

01
Adaptive spacing
Words surface exactly when your memory needs reinforcing — not on a fixed schedule. Your forgetting curve, accounted for.
02
Native-voice immersion
Every lesson uses real speaker audio recorded in-country. No synthesized voices, no approximations.
03
Context before grammar
You learn grammar by encountering it in real sentences, the same way a child does — not from textbook rules.
04
Live tutor sessions
One-on-one conversation practice with vetted native speakers. Scheduled in seconds, available in every timezone.
